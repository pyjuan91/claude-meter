# Privacy Policy — Claude Meter

**Last updated:** March 5, 2026

## Data Collection

Claude Meter does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## What the extension stores locally

- Your Claude.ai organization ID (to fetch usage data)
- Cached usage statistics (utilization percentages and reset times)

This data is stored in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) and is never sent to any external server.

## Third-party services

The extension only communicates with `claude.ai` — the site you are already logged into. It does not contact any other servers or analytics services.

## Your control

You can clear all stored data at any time by removing the extension. No data persists after uninstallation.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at [github.com/pyjuan91/claude-meter](https://github.com/pyjuan91/claude-meter/issues).
